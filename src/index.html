About us

Galactitrek is a state-of-the-art space tourism agency. We specialize in getting you to your destination in style and comfort. Our team of experts will help you plan your voyage and make sure you get to explore the galaxy in the most exciting and thrilling way possible. We offer a variety of packages and services to make sure you get the most out of your experience. Whether you are looking for a weekend getaway to Mars in a C-Class ship or a month long trip to the Trappist system in our finest ship, Odyssey of the Stars, Galactitrek has something for you.

Our history

Founded in the year 2256 by Nathan Mills, Bjoern Petersen, and Nathan Feiler as a private space faring company for cargo delivery we soon rebranded into commercial space flight for everyday citizens in 2261. After suffering several setbacks (such as the Challenger2 disaster and losing government funding) we became the second leading space tourism company, behind SpaceX. After the 2nd Cold War began in 2697 we regained our government funding from the US and were able to massively expand. After the 2nd Cold War ended in 2715 we were able to then open up our space tourism even further to become within reach of extremely filthy wealthy people for what is to them, low costs. After we gained funding from the United Nations in 2730 we were able to expand even further to the extremely wealthy people, which leads us to today where we are the leading space tourism company in the world, with SpaceX in a close second place.
